Protein Fluff

Description

Ingredients

  • 25g protein powder of your choice
  • 5g Xanthan Gum
  • 280ml diet cream soda

Optional toppings:

  • Ice cream wafer
  • 99 flake

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the protein powder and xanthan gum and mix.
  2. Add one third of the diet cream soda and whisk for 2-3 minutes using an electric whisk. It's really important that you use an electric whisk here (you'll be whisking for an eternity if you do it by hand)
  3. Gradually pour in the remaining soda whilst whisking. Whisk for around 10 minutes in total and watch your fluff quadruple in size. Stick with it â€“ it will grow!
  4. Add a wafer and a flake, or your favourite toppings, or just spoon straight out of the bowl
