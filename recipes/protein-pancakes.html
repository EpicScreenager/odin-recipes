Protein Pancakes

Description

Ingredients

  • 30g protein powder of your choice
  • 30g oats
  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs>

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend until a thick consistency
  3. Fry on a medium heated pan until bubbles form on top
  4. Flip and cook until golden brown
  5. Remove from pan and top off with greek yogurt and blueberries
