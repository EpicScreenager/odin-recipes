Protein Pancakes

Description

Up your protein with these pancakes made with eggs, oats, milk and protein powder. A great breakfast after a workout, enjoy with nut butter or fruit

Ingredients

  • 30g protein powder of your choice
  • 30g oats
  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until a medium-thick consistency
  3. Fry on a medium heated pan until bubbles form on top
  4. Flip and cook until golden brown
  5. Remove from pan and top off with toppings of choice!
